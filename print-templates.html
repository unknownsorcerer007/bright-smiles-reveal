📋 How to Use These Templates
🧾 Bill Inserts
Print on regular paper. Cut along dotted lines. Staple or tape to the patient's bill/receipt. Each page has 3 inserts.
💊 Prescription Stickers
Print on sticker paper (Avery 5160 or similar). Cut into individual stickers. Stick on prescription bags or envelopes.
📄 Waiting Room Flyer
Print on cardstock for a counter stand, or regular paper for wall mounting. Can also be used as a table tent.
